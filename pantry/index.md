<span class="page-title">The Pantry</span>

<p>Opening the door of the pantry is a sensory explosion. The smokiness of the paprika mingles with the tang of the salt & vinegar chips as you reach in to grab the dark chocolate morsels for your famous chocolate chip cookies. If your pantry is anything like mine, it's filled with a multitude of ingredients, spices, and snacks in such a variety of flavor profiles that you wonder how a cohesive meal could ever come from it. But this is where the magic happens. <p>
